POPULAR DISHES

Azerbaijani cuisine is full of different types of fruits and vegetables,
such as aubergine, tomato, sweet pepper, spinach, cabbage, onion, sorrel, beet, radish, cucumber, green beans. Rice and products made from flour are widely used in national cuisine.

Kebab

A meat dish cooked on the grill, mainly bottled. However, kebabs are also made from vegetables, especially tomatoes, eggplant, peppers and potatoes.

Read More
Plov

Plov is one of the most widespread dishes in Azerbaijan, with more than 40 different recipes. Plovs have different names depending on the main ingredients accompanying rice.

Read More
Lavangi

Levengi or Lavangi is a national dish of Azerbaijan and features in Talysh. .It is a fish or chicken stuffed with walnuts, onions and various condiments and baked in the oven

Read More
Dolma

A special type of food, commonly called dolma, cooked using grape leaves. The composition of the leaf stuffing is slightly different from other stuffing.

Read More
Piti

Piti is an Azerbaijani soup in the cuisines of the South Caucasus, its bordering nations, and Central Asia, and is prepared in the oven in individual crocks with a glazed interior.
